The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana


The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) has introduced a significant reform starting from the Kharif 2024 season by implementing a 12% penalty for insurance companies that delay claim settlements beyond the stipulated period.
